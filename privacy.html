Privacy Policy

Versets Clairs Publisher is used by its owner to publish prepared short-form videos to TikTok. This policy explains what information is used when the TikTok integration is enabled.

Information We Use

When the owner connects TikTok, the app may receive basic TikTok profile information such as an open ID, display name, and avatar. The app also uses the video files and captions selected by the owner for upload or publication.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to authenticate the owner, upload selected videos, and publish or prepare content through TikTok's Content Posting API. The app does not sell personal data, run advertising profiles, or scrape TikTok content.

Sharing

Selected videos and captions are sent to TikTok only when the owner authorizes the action. Data is not shared with unrelated third parties.

Data Retention

Local project files remain under the owner's control. TikTok authorization tokens are used only for the integration and should be removed if access is no longer needed.

Contact

For privacy questions about this app, contact the app owner through the TikTok developer account associated with this application.
