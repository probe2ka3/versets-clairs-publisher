What the app does

The app is designed for a single creator workflow. Videos are created locally, reviewed by the owner, then uploaded to TikTok only after the owner signs in and chooses to publish.

TikTok Login

Login Kit is used to let the owner authorize access to their TikTok account.

Content Posting

The Content Posting API is used for authorized video upload and Direct Post.

No scraping

The app does not scrape TikTok, read other users' data, or publish silently.
